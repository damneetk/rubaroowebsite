Rubaroo - simply meaning to come face to face, with myself, society and issues around me, which include - my choices, my values, my fears, and everything that becomes a part of my identity. It is a space, which nurtures potential to challenge and breakaway from the conditioning of society but also a space that gives us the freedom to choose consciously, question, and learn to be.

Taking from this meaning, Rubaroo aims to be an organization where adoloscents and young people are empowered to co-create a space to understand their 'Self' better, understand issues in society and take action. The space inspires them to nourish relationships, and become socially conscious leaders in their own capacity.





Why

We, at Rubaroo aim to create an inclusive world which is empathetic about the world. We believe that change begins by finding/co-creating spaces where individuals are enabled to challenge and question all that they have known or have been told by others about themselves and the world around them. It is important to do so because only by breaking down their own ‘stories’, and challenging themselves, that they can unleash within them the leadership to become conscious decision makers.

Who

We’re a team of Instructional Designers and Facilitators. Our skills lie in co-designing and contextualizing programmes along with partner institutes and working with them to understand the needs of their participating group.

How

We believe learning happens with our interaction with the world. Therefore, we create experiential based learning for young people through meaningful interactions. For us the ‘world’ can be interaction with opposite gender, caste, religion any other identity, with whom we would not have had the opportunity to understand their ‘story’. These learning spaces are where young people can come together to learn, to teach, and to unlearn; to have fun, take action and become proactive participants in their communities; by coming face to face with themselves and the world around them.




Issues


Early & Child Marriage

A year long program focuses on early and child marriage. It aims to work in building the dreams and aspiration of adolescents and supports in their capacity to negotiate and take decisions which facilitates their leadership journey.

Sustainable Development

A 3-4 week program which is done through the medium of rural exposure and internship with grass-root level organizations. Young people decode what development means through systems-thinking.

Interfaith

A program which works with young people on decoding what faith means and challenges stereotypes and misconception which one has about the 'others'. The aim is to celebrate diversity and promote community cohesion.

Campaign

Rubaroo partners with Pravah and Commutiny - The Youth Collective in running National level campaigns which engages youth on various thematic like democracy, cross border friendship, active citizenship, gender.

Gender

Through this we break down the basic words like difference between 'sex' and 'gender' and how we knowingly and unknowingly keep conforming to gender norms. We facilitate in decoding that gender is not just women issues it includes all gender based identity.

Leadership

At Rubaroo leadership does not mean leading others, we believe that anyone and everyone can be a leader in their own lives. For us leader is one who is inclusive in their action and contributes to change in any space.
