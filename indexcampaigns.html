Samvidhan LIVE!







The Jagrik Project is a campaign aimed at building young people’s capacities in learning about the Indian constitution experientially. A 2-month campaign which starts on November 26th- The Indian Constitution Day and culminates as a National Level Programme on 26th January- Indian Republic Day. Young people, participate and lead tasks based on the Fundamental Rights and Duties. The participating Youth becomes a Jagrik - An Awakened Citizen.


Our past experience so far ..

The program has so far reached out to more than 100 youth and adolescents , The campaign is led by ComMutiny - The Youth Collective, Delhi and regionally more than 26 organizations across India are a part of this public initiative.

Want to run this programme in your Institution?

This programme can be integrated during Academic hours and becomes an interesting module for citizenship and understanding the Constitution and role of citizen.




Rubaroo with Gender








Every year, the United Nation observes 25th November, the International Day for the Elimination of Violence against Women, to 10th December, Human Rights Day, as 16 Days of Activism against Gender-Based Violence. The campaign is a time to galvanize action to end violence against women and girls around the world.




To express our solidarity with the world wide campaign, Rubaroo has been running, Rubaroo with Gender campaign in Hyderabad since the past 4 years. Every year we host the campaign in a Public space of Hyderabad which focuses on various themes like Girl Child Marriage, Gender Roles, Gender Based Violence In Marriage and Gender based discrimination and stereotypes in everyday live. During the campaign, we host Film Screening alongside an Open Forum Discussion,Interactive Activity Booths And Social Experiments exploring the theme of Gender Based Violence, Early Child Marriages, Marriage And Partnership and facilitate conversations on gender vis a vis equality, identity, violence and basic human rights.

Our past experience so far ..

The programme has reached out to more than 500 youth directly and more than 1000s indirectly. British Deputy High Commision. Ajws, ARROW, Pravah, MANUU have collaborated with Rubaroo in the past to to run Rubaroo with Gender campaign.

Want to run this programme in your Institution?

A great module to help decode Gender studies and participants build more understanding by running Social action Projects to sensitize larger audience. The exhibition comes our very interactive and with lot of learning.
